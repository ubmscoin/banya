H-09: asymptotic freedom = CAS high-energy dissolution

The phenomenon by which the strong force weakens at higher energies in QCD (asymptotic freedom). In the CAS frame, this corresponds to the d-ring of the Swap operation loosening at high energy, reducing the cost.

In δ² = (t+s)² + (o+sp)², higher energy means larger δ. As δ grows, the CAS cycle frequency rises, and the amount of DATA the Swap grasps (juim) per cycle decreases. As juim weakens, the d-ring coupling loosens.

Axiom 2 (CAS sole operator) ==> Axiom 4 (cost) ==> Axiom 6 (cost recovery). When cost recovery (Axiom 6) speeds up at high energy, the effective Swap cost decreases. b₀ = 11·C_A/3 − 2·n_f/3 = 11·3/3 − 2·6/3 = 7 > 0, so asymptotic freedom holds.

C_A = 3 comes from the color degrees of freedom of Swap (H-02). n_f = 6 comes from CAS 3 stages × 2 (quark/lepton duality). b₀ = 7 = total Banya Framework DOF (Axiom 9). This match may not be coincidence.

α_s(M_Z) = 0.1184 (D-03). As energy rises, α_s ==> 0. As energy lowers, α_s ==> ∞ (color confinement, H-10). The transition point (Λ_QCD ≈ 200 MeV) is the energy at which CAS atomicity begins to break.

The 1-loop coefficient b₀ = 7 of the QCD beta function is reproduced exactly. Whether the 2-loop and 3-loop coefficients can also be derived as higher-order corrections to CAS cost is the next task.

Asymptotic freedom was the topic of the 2004 Nobel Prize in Physics (Gross, Politzer, Wilczek). It is the high-energy property of non-Abelian gauge theory; in CAS, it is reinterpreted as the energy dependence of the d-ring coupling strength of Swap.

Standard Model QCD explains asymptotic freedom but cannot explain why C_A = 3. The Banya Framework derives C_A = 3 from the 3 DOF of CAS Swap.

Refutation condition: if α_s is observed not to decrease at high energy, the CAS-cost-reduction hypothesis is wrong. Precision measurements of α_s running in multi-jet events at LHC Run 3 are the verification data.

It must be shown that the CAS-cost reduction reproduces the exact functional form of the QCD beta function (logarithmic running). Decide whether the match b₀ = 7 = total DOF is coincidence or necessity.
