Banya formula: $\tau_\tau = 192\pi^3/(G_F^2 m_\tau^5) \times B_e = 2.903 \times 10^{-13}\;\text{s}$. Substituting $m_\tau = 1776.86$ MeV and $B_e \approx 0.1782$ (electron-channel branching ratio).
