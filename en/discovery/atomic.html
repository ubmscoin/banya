Question: Why These Values?

Physics has numbers called "fundamental constants." Why the proton is 1836 times heavier than the electron, why the Bohr radius is 0.529 angstroms, why the electron's magnetic moment deviates from the Dirac value by 0.1%. The Standard Model takes these numbers as inputs. It does not compute them. "Why 1836?" is unresolved.
