$$\text{domain time (bit 2)}\neq\text{CAS (bit 4,5,6) measurement tool}$$

(4) Structural consequence: in physics "measuring time" means reading the change in domain time. But this is the result (output) of CAS cycles, not the CAS cycle itself. The execution time of CAS cannot be measured by domain time.
