(1) Top-quark decay width Γ_t = G_F m_t³/(8π√2) = 1.35 GeV. Top is the maximum-cost CAS Swap state.

(7) Physics correspondence: the top quark is the only quark that does not hadronize. CAS Swap is so fast that decay occurs before the FSM 111 (confinement) forms. This is the "bare quark".
