Banya formula: $\Delta m_s = 17.765 \pm 0.006\;\text{ps}^{-1}$. $\Delta m_s \propto G_F^2 m_t^2 |V_{ts}|^2 f_{B_s}^2 B_{B_s} m_{B_s}/(6\pi^2)$. It is the oscillation frequency of the CAS 3rd-generation Swap.

Numerics: prediction $17.765\;\text{ps}^{-1}$, experiment $17.765 \pm 0.006\;\text{ps}^{-1}$. Matches within error. First measured by CDF (2006) and refined at LHCb.
