H-847: Memory = CAS Read/Write on Discrete DATA Storage

$$\text{DATA}[addr] \xrightarrow{\text{CAS read}} \text{output} \;;\quad \text{input} \xrightarrow{\text{CAS write}} \text{DATA}[addr]$$

Grade: B

Axiom 4 (DATA discrete), Axiom 5 (CAS read/write), Axiom 2 (ECS address lattice).
