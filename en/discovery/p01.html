P-01: neutrino mass sum

P-01 prediction (predictions.html#r1)

Starting point: the Banya formula $\delta^2 = (\text{time} + \text{space})^2 + (\text{observer} + \text{superposition})^2$. Every particle is the output of a CAS operation. CAS has three stages: Read, Compare, Swap.

Electron, muon, and tau pass through all three CAS stages. They are charged leptons. They carry charge. At the Compare stage they couple to the electromagnetic force.

The neutrino is different. It skips the Compare stage. It bypasses Compare and goes straight to Swap. So it carries no charge. It does not couple to the electromagnetic force. It interacts only via the weak force.

Skipping Compare means that an $\alpha$-worth of coupling is omitted. This is why the neutrino mass is so extremely small.

The cost of skipping Compare appears as a power of $\alpha$. When Compare is bypassed in the CAS phase space, a 5th-order suppression arises.

Why 5th order? The dimension of the CAS phase space is 4 domain + 3 internal DOF = 7. The DOF occupied by Compare is 5 out of 7. The Compare operation runs across the 4 domains and one internal DOF is attached on top. Since all 5 of these are dropped, the suppression is $\alpha^5$.

Decomposition: Compare domain crossing (comparing Status across all 4 domains) + verdict residual (1 internal DOF for the compare verdict) = 5. The remaining 7-5=2 are Read (1) + minimal existence condition (1), which matches the physical fact that the neutrino interacts only via the weak force (Read).

A correction factor of $3/2$ attaches to the bare $\alpha^5$ suppression. Since 1 of the 3 CAS stages (Compare) is skipped, the correction is carried by the remaining 2 stages (Read, Swap). With 2 of 3 stages surviving, the factor is $3/2$.

The current cosmological upper bound is $\Sigma < 120\;\text{meV}$. The Banya Framework prediction $58.5\;\text{meV}$ sits inside this range. It is also consistent with the $\Delta m^2$ values from oscillation experiments. With $\Delta m^2_{32} \sim 2.5 \times 10^{-3}\;\text{eV}^2$ and $55.2^2 - 3.3^2 = 3036\;\text{meV}^2 \sim 3.0 \times 10^{-3}\;\text{eV}^2$, the order of magnitude agrees.
