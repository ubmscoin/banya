Question: Why these values

Black hole thermodynamics, the degeneracy-pressure index, Dirac's large number, the matter-radiation equality redshift -- each has been treated as an independent physical phenomenon. The Banya Framework sees these as different domain projections of one and the same CAS (Compare-And-Swap) cost structure. The cost incurred by a single write, when the domain is switched, becomes a temperature, an exponent, a large number, or a redshift.
