$R_l = \Gamma_{\text{had}}/\Gamma_{\text{lep}} = 20.83$. Hadron channels are multiplied by CAS 3 stages (color factor 3) and the $\alpha_s$ QCD correction is added.

Axiom 3 (CAS 3 stages) supplies the color factor $3$. D-03 ($\alpha_s$) determines the QCD correction. Axiom 2 (CAS sole operator) supplies the coupling strength of each quark channel.
