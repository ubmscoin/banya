H-356: Single-axis active patterns + adjacent pairs = 6 = lepton generations

$$\{0001,0010,0100,1000\}+\{0011,1100\}=6$$

(1) Among the 16 domain patterns, the 4 single-bit-ON patterns ({0001, 0010, 0100, 1000}) plus the 2 adjacent-pair-ON patterns ({0011, 1100}) sum to 6. This corresponds to the number of lepton generations.

(2) Banya formula: {0001, 0010, 0100, 1000} + {0011, 1100} = 6. The sum of single-axis excitations and intra-bracket adjacent-pair excitations.

(3) By Axiom 1 (4 orthogonal axes) the 4-bit domain forms 2⁴=16 patterns. By Axiom 15 (δ) these patterns are active only when δ=1. Single-axis and adjacent-pair states are the minimal excitations.
