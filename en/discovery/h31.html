Structural correspondence. Right-handed neutrinos are not observed in any experiment. Even if they existed, they would not participate in Standard-Model interactions (sterile).

The left-right symmetric model predicts that right-handed interactions are restored at high energy. If CAS irreversibility is absolute, right-handed interactions do not exist at any energy.

If a right-handed neutrino (or sterile neutrino) were discovered, there would be an exception to CAS irreversibility, or the γ⁵ = CAS 1 cycle correspondence would be wrong. Sterile-neutrino searches are under way at SHiP, DUNE, etc.

The mathematical isomorphism between γ⁵ and the CAS 1 cycle must be proved. At present this is only a structural similarity. It must be rigorously derived that chirality arises from the irreversibility of the CAS FSM.
