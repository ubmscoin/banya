Round 1. CAS Reading of the Koide Formula

Yoshio Koide discovered an empirical formula in 1982: a curious relation among the three lepton masses (electron, muon, tau).

Why exactly $2/3$? Koide himself could not explain it. The puzzle remained unresolved for over 40 years. This round runs the Banya Framework 5-step procedure to find the answer.
