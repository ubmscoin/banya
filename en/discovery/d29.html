Norm substitution: 19 = number of Standard Model free parameters. This is the total count of independent degrees of freedom the CAS workbench must describe. 3 = number of CAS stages. Distributing 19 parameters across 3 stages gives an average of 19/3 degrees of freedom per stage.

Axiom chain: Axiom 2 ($2^N$ shift) ==> Axiom 6 (CAS atomicity) ==> Axiom 9 (cost). Raising $\alpha$ to the 19/3 power is the total cost when CAS processes 19 free parameters across 3 stages.

Numerical: $M_\text{GUT} = 91.1876 \times 137.036^{19/3} \approx 10^{16}$ GeV.

Error: within the experimentally inferred GUT scale range $\sim 10^{16}$ GeV. The precise GUT scale is indirectly constrained by proton-decay searches.
